- [ ] All testers' Google accounts are added to the Closed Testing
      tester list in Play Console
- [ ] The opt-in URL above resolves correctly
      (test it in incognito browser first)

      `eas update --branch production --message "Notif sync + optimistic saves"`

      catch any onboarding issues fast (first-day friction
      is the biggest churn driver)

## After-send tracking

Keep a quick spreadsheet (or just a note) of:
- Who got the invite
- Who opened it
- Who installed
- Who completed onboarding
- Who created a status
- Any bugs reported

Google needs 12 active testers for 14 consecutive days before
Production access. Activity matters more than headcount —
better 8 active than 11 silent.
